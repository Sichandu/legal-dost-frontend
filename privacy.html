Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 25, 2026

Your Privacy Matters: This Privacy Policy explains how Legal Dost collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Documents You Upload

When you use Legal Dost, you voluntarily upload legal documents (images or PDFs). These documents:

  • Are processed by our AI to extract text and provide analysis
  • May contain sensitive personal or business information
  • Are sent to Google's Gemini AI service for processing
  • Are NOT permanently stored on our servers (currently)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect:

  • IP address and location data
  • Browser type and device information
  • Usage statistics (pages visited, time spent)
  • Error logs and technical diagnostics

1.3 Information We DON'T Collect (Currently)

During beta phase, we do NOT collect:

  • Email addresses or contact information
  • Payment information (Service is currently free)
  • User accounts or registration data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide the Service: Process your documents and generate AI analysis
  • Improve the Service: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Fix Issues: Debug errors and improve system reliability
  • Understand Users: Learn what notice types are most common
  • Comply with Law: Meet legal obligations if required

3. Third-Party Services

Service Purpose Data Shared
Google Gemini AI AI text analysis and generation Uploaded documents, extracted text
Hosting Provider Host our application IP address, usage logs
Analytics (future) Track usage statistics Anonymous usage data

Google Gemini Privacy: When we send your documents to Google Gemini for analysis, Google's privacy policy also applies. Read more at: Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Retention

Current Practice (Beta Phase):

  • Uploaded documents are processed in real-time
  • We do NOT permanently store your documents
  • AI responses may be temporarily cached
  • Analytics data is retained for service improvement

Future (When We Add Accounts):

  • Registered users may have analysis history stored
  • Users can request data deletion at any time
  • Inactive accounts will be deleted after 12 months

5. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted
  • Secure Hosting: We use reputable cloud providers
  • Access Controls: Limited personnel access to systems
  • Regular Updates: Software kept up-to-date with security patches

However: No system is 100% secure. Do not upload highly sensitive documents if you cannot accept any risk of breach. We recommend using lawyer consultation for truly confidential matters.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Know what data we have about you (when we add accounts)
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Opt-Out: Stop using the Service at any time
  • Portability: Receive your data in portable format (future)

7. Children's Privacy

Legal Dost is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If you believe a child has used our Service, please contact us.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Current: We do NOT use cookies for tracking.

Future: When we add analytics or user accounts, we may use:

  • Session cookies (necessary for functionality)
  • Analytics cookies (to understand usage)
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings

9. International Users

Legal Dost is operated in India. If you access from outside India:

  • Your data may be transferred to and processed in India
  • Indian data protection laws apply
  • You consent to this transfer by using the Service

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our Service evolves. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use after changes means you accept the new policy.

11. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your information:

  • We will investigate immediately
  • Affected users will be notified (when we have contact info)
  • We will take steps to prevent future breaches

12. Your Consent

By using Legal Dost, you consent to:

  • Collection and use of information as described in this policy
  • Processing of your documents by third-party AI services
  • Transfer of data as necessary to provide the Service

13. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests:

Email: privacy@legaldost.in (coming soon)
Subject: "Privacy Inquiry"

14. Compliance

Legal Dost strives to comply with:

  • India's Information Technology Act, 2000
  • Personal Data Protection Bill (when enacted)
  • Industry best practices for data protection

Important Reminder: While we protect your data, you should NOT upload documents containing extremely sensitive information unless you've evaluated the risks and have no better alternative.
